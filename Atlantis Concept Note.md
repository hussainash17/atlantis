# **Atlantis**

## **AI-Powered Multi-Asset Investment Platform**

---

### **Executive Summary**

Atlantis represents a comprehensive, AI-driven investment intelligence and execution platform engineered to serve as the primary retail investment infrastructure for Bangladesh and comparable emerging markets. The platform democratizes access to capital markets by enabling individuals—regardless of financial literacy—to discover, analyze, and execute investments across diverse regulated financial instruments through a unified digital interface.

**Structural Positioning**: Atlantis functions as financial market infrastructure rather than a product issuer. All investment instruments remain owned, issued, and regulated by licensed third-party institutions, including banks, brokerages, fintech providers, agri-fintech platforms, commodity providers, and asset managers. Atlantis serves exclusively as the distribution, intelligence, and execution layer, integrated through secure API architecture.

**Platform Capabilities**: The solution combines institutional-grade analytics, adaptive robo-advisory intelligence, brokerage-level execution capability, and wallet-based transparency—specifically calibrated for emerging market conditions, accessible minimum investments (approximately USD 9–10), and full regulatory compliance.

---

### **Market Problem Statement**

Despite the proliferation of regulated financial instruments, retail participation in capital markets remains structurally constrained by systemic barriers:

* **Fragmented Infrastructure**: Investment access dispersed across multiple institutions and applications  
* **Limited Decision Support**: Execution-focused platforms lacking analytical and advisory capabilities  
* **Access Barriers**: Prohibitively high minimum investment thresholds  
* **Knowledge Gap**: Insufficient financial literacy coupled with institutional trust deficits  
* **Portfolio Opacity**: Absence of unified wallet architecture and consolidated portfolio visualization

**Market Impact**: These structural inefficiencies result in over USD 170 billion in household savings remaining allocated to low-yield deposit instruments, representing a significant capital allocation inefficiency with material implications for long-term wealth creation.

---

### **Solution Architecture**

Atlantis delivers an end-to-end investment journey structured across seven integrated modules:

1. **Onboarding & Profiling** — Digital KYC, risk tolerance assessment, investment goal calibration  
2. **Education & Discovery** — Plain-language financial education and AI-curated learning pathways  
3. **AI-Powered Analysis** — Probabilistic forecasting, scenario modeling, risk-adjusted insights with 95% confidence intervals  
4. **Decision Support** — Explainable, personalized investment recommendations  
5. **Transaction Execution** — Single-click buy/sell capability via licensed third-party APIs  
6. **Wallet & Portfolio Management** — Real-time balance tracking, holdings management, performance analytics  
7. **Exit & Liquidity** — Streamlined selling process and settlement management

**User Experience Objective**: Enable first-time investors to execute informed investment decisions within minutes of onboarding.

---

### **Asset Class Coverage**

Atlantis provides comprehensive access to all major regulated retail instruments available in Bangladesh and comparable emerging markets:

* **Equities & Exchange-Traded Funds** — Listed stocks and ETFs (via licensed broker integration)  
* **Fixed Income Securities** — Government bonds, corporate bonds, Sukuk instruments  
* **Savings Products** — Bank savings accounts, term deposits, digital savings instruments  
* **Collective Investment Schemes** — Equity funds, debt funds, balanced funds, Shariah-compliant funds  
* **Precious Metals & Commodities** — Digital gold, commodity-backed investment instruments  
* **Agri-Fintech Products** — Warehouse receipt-backed investments, crop-linked financing, input financing with structured returns  
* **Structured & Alternative Products** — Capital-protected instruments and thematic products (subject to regulatory approval)

**Important**: Atlantis neither custodies assets nor issues financial products.

### **Target Market Segments**

**Primary Segment**: Retail individuals aged 21–55, predominantly urban and semi-urban demographics, mobile-first users with zero to intermediate financial literacy.

**Secondary Segments**: Small and medium enterprises managing surplus liquidity; expatriate Bangladeshis (subject to regulatory framework).

### **Regulatory Framework**

Atlantis operates under a technology and distribution platform model characterized by:

* Zero asset issuance  
* Zero balance-sheet exposure  
* Zero asset custody responsibility  
* Progressive licensing acquisition aligned with regulatory expansion  
* Full compliance with Bangladesh Securities and Exchange Commission (BSEC), Bangladesh Bank, Anti-Money Laundering (AML), Know Your Customer (KYC), and data protection standards

### **Revenue Model**

The platform employs a diversified revenue architecture:

* Transaction-based commissions (revenue-sharing with product partners)  
* Platform fees calculated as basis points on Assets Under Management (AUM)  
* Premium AI analytics and advanced features subscription tier  
* B2B distribution and white-label licensing fees

**Target Blended Take Rate**: 0.6–0.9% annually on AUM
